PageTurners

PageTurners is dedicated to providing underprivileged children with access to books and educational resources. Our mission is to foster a love for reading and learning, empowering children to achieve their full potential.

What we do Explanation
  • Book Donations

PageTurners donates books to local libraries, Schools. However most of the time our business consists of handing out the books where they are needed most.
  • Reading Programs

Our reading programs are designed to teach beginners how to practice reading. PageTurners offers a free curriculum of reading books in order of skill level, and even offers the materials for the program to keep and use.
  • Educational Workshops

We hope to place Educational around the world, there will be classes teaching people how to begin reading and will dive into different forms of literature. This experience is specifically designed to spark interest in the underprivileged youth.
